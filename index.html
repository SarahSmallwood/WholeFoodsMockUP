Online Ordering

Online ordering is a convenient way to get everything you need from Whole Foods Market. Shop for groceries with Amazon Prime, get lunch and dinner to go in our app, order catering for your next event, and even get gift cards to share with your loved ones. Check out all the ways you can order online.

Grocery Pickup or Delivery

Prime members can shop Whole Foods Market on Amazon.com for convenient delivery and free pickup in select ZIP codes.

Catering and Everyday Entertaining

Order meals, entrées and party must-haves online, and then pick them up at the store.

Lunch and Dinner To-Go

Use the Whole Foods Market app to order hot pizza and other freshly prepared favorites for pickup or delivery starting at $2.99.

Gift Cards

Treat your favorite food lover to a Whole Foods Market gift card. Send a virtual gift card via text or email, or ship a physical card.
